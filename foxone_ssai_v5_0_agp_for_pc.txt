! *** FIX IN THIS VERSION ***
! v4.3 confirmed pre-rolls blocked successfully. Mid-rolls
! were causing NETWORK_ERROR and stopping playback entirely.
!
! Root cause: creatives block rule was dropping the TCP
! connection (status=0 in AGP log). Bitmovin receives a
! sudden connection refusal mid-stream and throws
! NETWORK_ERROR ‚Äî it has no graceful handling for a dead
! connection during active media pipeline playback.
!
! Pre-rolls skip cleanly because Bitmovin has higher
! tolerance for segment failures before content begins.
! Mid-rolls are catastrophic ‚Äî player is already in a
! running pipeline and connection refusal breaks it.
!
! Fix: redirect=nooptext instead of hard block.
! AGP returns HTTP 200 with empty body. Bitmovin receives
! a valid response, attempts to decode zero bytes as
! MPEG-TS, gets empty/corrupt frame, and advances past
! the segment rather than throwing NETWORK_ERROR.
!
! The single changed lines vs v4.3:
!   BEFORE: .../creatives/*$important
!   AFTER:  .../creatives/*$redirect=nooptext,important
! ============================================================
